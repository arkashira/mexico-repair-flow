## Customer Segments
- Independent auto repair shops in Mexico (≈ 12,000 shops)  
- Small‑to‑medium garage chains (2–5 locations) seeking standardized quoting  
- Fleet managers for local logistics companies that outsource maintenance to local shops  
- OEM service networks looking for a white‑label quoting layer in the Mexican market  

## Value Propositions
- **Instant digital quotes** generated from VIN, make/model, and labor tables → 30 % faster than paper estimates.  
- **End‑to‑end workflow** (quote → approval → parts ordering → invoicing) reduces re‑work errors by ~25 %.  
- **Localized compliance** (Mexican tax codes, CFDI generation) eliminates costly manual invoicing penalties.  
- Mobile‑first UI in Spanish with offline mode for shops with spotty internet.  
- Data analytics dashboard for shop owners to track technician productivity and parts margins.  

## Channels
- Direct sales via a dedicated Mexican‑market SDR team (phone & WhatsApp).  
- Partnerships with local parts distributors (e.g., Grupo Quetzal) who bundle the SaaS with their ERP.  
- Marketplace listing on **MercadoLibre Servicios** and **Google Play Store** (Android app).  
- Referral program: 10 % discount on next month’s subscription for each shop‑to‑shop referral.  

## Customer Relationships
- Dedicated onboarding specialist (first 30 days) → 90 % adoption rate.  
- In‑app chat support (Spanish) with SLA ≤ 2 h.  
- Quarterly business reviews (QBR) for fleet managers to align on KPIs.  
- Community forum & knowledge base hosted on a localized sub‑domain (mx.axentx.com).
