 # marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
1-line: Streamline your auto repair shop with Mexico's first digital quoting and workflow management tool, **Mexico Repair Flow**.

Alternatives:
1. Manual paperwork and phone calls (inefficient, prone to errors)
2. Existing generic workflow management tools (lack of industry-specific features, high cost)
3. DIY solutions (time-consuming, lack of support and scalability)

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
### Persona 1: Juan Gonzalez, Shop Owner, Budget: $500/month
- Daily Job: Overseeing operations, managing staff, and ensuring customer satisfaction.
- Pain Points: Inefficient manual processes, errors in quotes, and difficulty tracking work orders.

### Persona 2: Maria Rodriguez, Workshop Manager, Budget: $300/month
- Daily Job: Coordinating repairs, managing inventory, and liaising with customers.
- Pain Points: Time-consuming manual processes, difficulty in tracking work progress, and errors in quotes.

### Persona 3: Carlos Martinez, Technician, Budget: $100/month
- Daily Job: Performing repairs and maintenance tasks.
- Pain Points: Inefficient communication with the workshop, delays in work orders, and errors in quotes.

## Channels
1. LinkedIn Ads (CAC estimate: $200) - Target auto repair shop owners and managers in Mexico.
2. Google Ads (CAC estimate: $150) - Target keywords related to auto repair, workflow management, and digital solutions in Mexico.
3. Local Auto Repair Shop Events (CAC estimate: $50) - Sponsor and attend local auto repair shop events to build relationships and generate leads.

## Content Cadence
Week 1:
- Launch teaser on LinkedIn and Twitter.
- Blog post: "Transform your auto repair shop with Mexico Repair Flow."

Week 2:
- Webinar: "Streamline your auto repair shop with Mexico Repair Flow."
- Case study: Success story of a Mexican auto repair shop using Mexico Repair Flow.

Week 3:
- Email campaign to targeted leads.
- Video testimonial from a satisfied customer.

Week 4:
- Follow-up email to leads who showed interest but didn't convert.
- Blog post: "5 ways Mexico Repair Flow can revolutionize your auto repair shop."

## Launch Milestones
- D-30: Finalize content, prepare for webinar, and start email campaign.
- D-0: Launch Mexico Repair Flow, webinar, and content.
- D+30: Follow-up email to leads, publish case study, and video testimonial.
- D+90: Evaluate success metrics and adjust strategy if necessary.

## Success Metrics
- DAU (Daily Active Users): 50 by D+30, 100 by D+90.
- $MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) target by D+90: $15,000.
