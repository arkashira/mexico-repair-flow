# Partner Targets & Integration Roadmap

## Integration Roadmap

### Phase 1: Core Integrations (0-3 months)
1. **QuickBooks Online**
   - **Rationale**: Essential for financial management and invoicing.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: 50 transactions/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium.
   - **Value-Add**: Automates invoicing and financial tracking for shop owners.

2. **Google Maps API**
   - **Rationale**: Critical for location-based services and customer directions.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: $200 monthly credit.
   - **Integration Effort**: Small.
   - **Value-Add**: Enhances customer experience by providing accurate directions to the repair shop.

### Phase 2: Enhanced Functionality (3-6 months)
3. **Zapier**
   - **Rationale**: Enables seamless integration with a wide range of third-party apps.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: 100 tasks/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium.
   - **Value-Add**: Allows for custom workflows and automations, improving efficiency.

4. **Twilio**
   - **Rationale**: Essential for SMS and voice notifications.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: 10,000 SMS/month, 1,000 voice minutes/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium.
   - **Value-Add**: Enhances communication with customers through automated notifications.

### Phase 3: Advanced Features (6-12 months)
5. **Shopify**
   - **Rationale**: Useful for shops that also sell parts online.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: 50 transactions/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Large.
   - **Value-Add**: Streamlines inventory management and online sales.

6. **Salesforce**
   - **Rationale**: Advanced CRM capabilities for larger repair shops.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: 10,000 API calls/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Large.
   - **Value-Add**: Improves customer relationship management and sales tracking.

7. **Stripe**
   - **Rationale**: Secure payment processing.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: No free tier, but low transaction fees.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium.
   - **Value-Add**: Facilitates secure and efficient payment processing.

8. **Calendly**
   - **Rationale**: Scheduling and appointment management.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: 1 calendar/location.
   - **Integration Effort**: Small.
   - **Value-Add**: Simplifies appointment scheduling for both customers and shop staff.

## Prioritization
- **High Priority**: QuickBooks Online, Google Maps API, Twilio (affiliate/revenue-share potential).
- **Medium Priority**: Zapier, Stripe (essential for core functionality).
- **Low Priority**: Shopify, Salesforce, Calendly (enhanced features for larger shops).
