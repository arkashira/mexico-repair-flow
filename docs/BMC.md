# Business Model Canvas

## Value Proposition
Mexico Repair Flow is a simple customer information management system designed to help businesses in Mexico efficiently manage their customer data. Our system provides a user-friendly interface for creating, retrieving, updating, and searching customer information, making it an essential tool for any business looking to streamline their customer management processes.

### Key Features

*   Create, read, update, and delete (CRUD) customer information
*   Search customers by name
*   Save and load customer data from JSON files
*   Scalable and customizable for businesses of all sizes

## Customer Segments
Mexico Repair Flow is designed for businesses in Mexico that require a simple and efficient customer information management system. Our target customer segments include:

*   Small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs)
*   E-commerce companies
*   Retail businesses
*   Service providers

## Channels
Mexico Repair Flow will be available through the following channels:

*   Web application
*   Mobile application (iOS and Android)
*   API for integration with other systems

## Revenue Streams
Mexico Repair Flow will generate revenue through the following streams:

*   Subscription-based model: Offer a monthly or annual subscription to access the full range of features
*   Customization and integration services: Offer customized solutions and integration services for businesses that require tailored solutions
*   Data analytics: Provide data analytics services to help businesses make informed decisions based on their customer data

## Cost Structure
The cost structure for Mexico Repair Flow includes:

*   Development and maintenance costs
*   Infrastructure and hosting costs
*   Marketing and advertising costs
*   Customer support and maintenance costs

## Key Resources
The key resources required for Mexico Repair Flow include:

*   Development team with expertise in Python, JavaScript, and database management
*   Infrastructure and hosting services
*   Marketing and advertising budget
*   Customer support and maintenance team

## Key Activities
The key activities required for Mexico Repair Flow include:

*   Development and maintenance of the system
*   Marketing and advertising efforts to reach target customer segments
*   Providing customer support and maintenance services
*   Continuously gathering feedback and improving the system

## Key Partners
The key partners required for Mexico Repair Flow include:

*   Infrastructure and hosting providers
*   Marketing and advertising agencies
*   Customer support and maintenance service providers
*   Partnerships with other businesses to offer bundled services and solutions
